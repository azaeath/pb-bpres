A Start, Unfinished

The story of my time as a graphic designer is not a long one, as I had originally spent my time working on my illustration skills. I got into graphic design primarily as a major intended to keep me in front of a computer, where I felt most comfortable with my artistic skills, around 2017, following my return from engineer school in the Marine Corps. Since then, I have not established a great body of completed work, as most things I do are personal projects for the sake of learning. However, the things that I have completed have all been client work and left them feeling satisfied with outcome of my efforts.
